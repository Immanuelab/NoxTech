French Dungeon 2: The Return of Croissant

Overview:

"French Dungeon 2: The Return of Croissant" is a text based game.

Your goal is to save your people in dungeon.

This is the second French Dungeon of the French Dungeon series.


Storyline:

You become child of main character in French Dungeon Origin.

You can be either Croissant (Male) or Ficelle (Female)

One day, they finds out their neighbor and their father are missing.

French T.Oasty the mayor of Pont-Évêque tells them that somebody kidnapped them into the dungeon.

They go into the dungeon to save them.


Detail:

This was started developing in 29th June 2020 and released in 21st November 2020.

This was planned to be made after Tom Bradley finishes the first French Dungeon.

Immanuel, Liam Goldingham and Tom Bradley participated in development.


System Requirements:

OS: Window 10 or above

CPU: Celeron E3500 or above

RAM: 250MB or above

Storage: 1MB or bigger

Input: Keyboard

French Dungeon Origin

Overview:

"French Dungeon Origin" is the text based RPG dungeon game on web platform.

The goal is to escape from the Dungeon and go back to the Town.

This was the first game of the French Dungeon series.


Storyline:

One guy settled down in a small town called Ascot.

Every townfolks loved his kindness to all.

He left his child and that child was nothing like him.

He was sent to the dungeon in France by townfolks because of his evil mind.

Now he has to survive from this dungeon and you will become him in the game.


Detail:

It was originally planned to be developed by 3 people.

Lachlan Torr, Tom Bradley and Immanuel have set the plan up but 2 of them left the group.

It is created by Tom Bradley in 2019.

It was also unaccessible due to hosting problem but restored the code from Immanuel using Github Page.

It went back to online in 2021 August.


System Requirements:

OS: Windows XP or later, Mac OS 10.12 Sierra or later

Browser: Google Chrome, Mozila Firefox, Microsoft Edge, Opera

Input: Keyboard
